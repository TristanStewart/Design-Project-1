Leg Excersises

All Four Parts of the Legs

The legs have four main muscle groups: the glutes in the buttocks help
with moving the hips, the quadriceps at the front of the thigh are
important for straightening the knee, the hamstrings at the back of
the thigh help with bending the knee and extending the hip, and the calf
muscles at the back of the lower leg aid in pointing the foot downward.

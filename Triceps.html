All Three Parts of the Tricep

The triceps brachii is a muscle in the upper arm with three parts:
The long head at the back, the lateral head on the outer side, and
the medial head on the inner side. The long head helps with shoulder
and arm extension, while the lateral and medial heads mainly contribute
to elbow extension.

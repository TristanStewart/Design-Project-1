All Three Parts of the Biceps

The biceps has three parts: the long head and short head, which come
from the shoulder blade, and the brachialis, which comes from the front
of the upper arm. The long and short heads work together for bending
the elbow and also help in lifting the arm. The brachialis is mainly for
bending the elbow.

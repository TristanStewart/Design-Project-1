Back Excersises

All Three Parts of the Back

The back has three main parts: the lats, upper back, and lower back.
The lats are big muscles running from the upper arm to the lower spine,
helping with shoulder movements. The upper back, between the neck
and ribs, includes muscles like the trapezius, contributing to posture
and shoulder actions. The lower back, below the thoracic spine, has
muscles like the erector spinae, providing support for bending,
twisting, and posture.
